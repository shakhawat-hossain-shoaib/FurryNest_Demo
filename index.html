About The Project

A Fusion of Minimalism and Technology

NordGlass was born from a desire to create a web experience that is both visually striking and calming. We believe that good design is unobtrusive and intuitive.

By combining the cool, muted tones of the Nord color palette with the modern, layered effect of glass morphism, we aim to build interfaces that are a pleasure to use.
